There are two main things I like to do
  • Think about the brain

    This interest manifests as an interest in developing neuroscience- and cognitive science-informed artificial neural network models.

    I hope that someday we will have models with high-level information processing capabilities that rival the brain's.

  • Help others navigate this world

    Throughout my life, I have found advice, guidance, and mentorship from those more experienced than myself to be invaluable to my success.

    Here, I hope to "pay it forward." I share what I've learned about things like applying to summer research as an undergraduate, developing effective time and task management system skills, etc.


This site houses a small bio about myself and a blog in which I will mostly talk about the above two things. You can also find my CV.
