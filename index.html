There are two main things I like to do
  • Think about the brain

    I think the brain is beautiful . It is the most sophisticated autonomous system known to existence. All technology and knowledge that we know is the result of brains operating on the world's information.

    My own attempt to understand the brain is, ultimately, a brain trying to understand, at least for me, itself. Just beautiful. You can learn more about my interest in the brain and how my brain intends to try to understand itself here.

  • Help others navigate this world

    Throughout my life, I have found advice, guidance, and mentorship from those more experienced than myself to be invaluable to my success.

    Here, I hope to "pay it forward." I share what I've learned about things like applying to summer research as an undergraduate, developing effective time and task management system skills, etc. You can find more here.
