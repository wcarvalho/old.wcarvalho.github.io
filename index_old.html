There are two main things I like to do
  • Discuss the brain

    I think writing is excellent for organizing my thoughts and teaching for testing my understanding.

    As I continue to explore how the brain functions, I will use this as a medium to try to communicate my progress and teach others what I learn.

  • Give advice

    Throughout my life, I have found advice, guidance, and mentorship from those more experienced than myself to be invaluable to my success.

    Here, I hope to "pay it forward." I share what I've learned about things like applying to summer research as an undergraduate, developing effective time and task management skills, etc.


This site houses a small bio about myself and a blog in which I will mostly talk about the above two things. You can also find my CV.
